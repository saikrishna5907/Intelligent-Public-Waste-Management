Innovative Way Of Waste Management System


TrashTalks is redefining the way waste is managed. Our solution combines ultrasonic fill-level Smart Sensors that monitor waste in real-time with the help of sophisticated software. TrashTalks provides both cities and businesses with analytics that can be used for data-driven decision making in the optimization of waste collection routes, pick-up frequencies, and vehicle loads.




Smart Waste Management System
